The TaMADOR consortium met in Bethesda, MD on December 1, 2022 for a symposium on advances in multiplex quantification of protein/peptide hormones and biomarkers involved in obesity and diabetes.

The meeting brought together consortium members to share progress on assay development, validation, and implementation across the participating institutions.
