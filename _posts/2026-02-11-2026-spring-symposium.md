**Targeted Mass Spectrometry Assays for Diabetes and Obesity Research (TaMADOR)**

The 2026 TaMADOR Spring Symposium will be held on May 14th from 8:30 AM to 4:10 PM at UW Medicine, South Lake Union (Brotman Auditorium, D Building, 850 Republican St, Seattle, WA 98109).

Topics include:
- History and purpose of TaMADOR
- Single-islet proteomics and islet immune responses in T1D
- Surrogate endpoint biomarkers for T1D interventions from a regulatory perspective
- Interlaboratory validation of LC-MS/MS assays for glucagon and oxyntomodulin
- Quantification of proinsulin and proglucagon processing products
- Clinical applications of prohormone ratios in type 1 diabetes
- Cross-laboratory validation of multiplexed LC-MS/MS assays
- Panel discussion on C-peptide harmonization, clinical utility, and reimbursement
