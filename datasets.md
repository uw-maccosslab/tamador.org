## About Panorama Public

[Panorama Public](https://panoramaweb.org/project/home/public/begin.view) is a public repository for targeted mass spectrometry assays and data. It is hosted by the MacCoss Lab at the University of Washington and supports data sharing for the proteomics community.

All TaMADOR data is available under open access terms to facilitate reproducibility and collaborative research.
